# India CPI Inflation Analysis (2013-2023)

## 📌 Project Overview
A data analysis project examining India's Consumer Price Index (CPI) trends over the last decade. The goal was to visualize historical peaks and understand the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on different household expenditure categories.

## 🚀 Key Findings
* **Historical Trends:** Tracked inflation movement from 2013 to 2023, identifying the 2022 supply chain peak.
* **COVID-19 Impact:** Observed a significant shift in inflation toward Food and Essential Services during lockdown periods.
* **Category Analysis:** Drilled down into the October 2022 spike to identify specific drivers in the food basket.
* **External Factors:** Analyzed the relationship between Global Crude Oil prices and domestic Transport costs.

## 🛠 Tools Used
* **Data Source:** MOSPI (Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation) for Crude Oil Price
* **Analysis & Visualization:** Microsoft Excel

## 📊 Dashboard Components
1. **10-Year Trend:** Long-term view of inflation movement.
2. **Basket Composition:** Current weightage of categories in the CPI.
3. **Pandemic Comparison:** Pre-vs-Post COVID inflation behavior.
4. **Monthly Drill-down:** Targeted look at 2022 price volatility.
5. **Correlation Plot:** Scatter analysis of Oil vs. Transport.

## 🧠 Methodology
1. **Data Cleaning:** Handled missing values and standardized date formats for a 10-year period.
2. **Calculations:** Used YoY and MoM growth formulas to identify real-time changes.
3. **Design:** Applied a minimalist layout with focused color coding to highlight key data points.
